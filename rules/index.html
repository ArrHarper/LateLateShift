Defensive Scoring

We use modified scoring for defense, designed to properly reward or penalize based on in-game impact, and limit the chance for a defense to score big in fantasy while getting blown out in real life.

This system penalizes less for the first few scores, to reflect that NFL defenses give up an average of ~22 points per game (as of '22), and then penalties get steeper with more points and yards allowed.
